What is Neko! CMS?

Neko! CMS a very simple Content Management System built using PHP and MySQL and Twitter Bootstrap as CSS Framework. With Neko! CMS you can add dynamically pages , posts and even customize your page : changing the name of your webpage,adding social media account like facebook,twitter so that your viewers can follow you or add in social media,add photo in your Neko!CMS profile.

Features of Neko! CMS

• SEO friendly urls • Share your post in social media like Facebook,Twitter and Google + • Easy to install . no need programming knowledge. Even you’re a newbie or not you can easily set up your own blog • Responsive design.can fit in different devices such tablets,smartphones,phablet (Thanks to bootstrap!)
